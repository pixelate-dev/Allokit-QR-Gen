Printing Tips

Use Quality Paper

Use waterproof or vinyl sticker paper for durability.

High Quality Print

Print in high-quality mode for sharp QR codes.

Print at 100% Scale

Do not scale or fit to page — actual size only.

Test Before Use

Scan the QR code after printing to verify it works.
